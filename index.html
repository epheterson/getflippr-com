Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled when you use Flippr ("the App").

What Information We Collect

Flippr is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, use, or share any personal information about you. All app data, including your flip history and statistics, is stored locally on your device.

Local Storage Only

All information generated by the app (such as coin flip results, statistics, and preferences) is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

No Analytics or Tracking

Flippr does not include any analytics, tracking, or advertising systems. We have no way to track your usage of the app or collect any information about you.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party services that might collect information about you.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's no personal information for you to access, modify, or delete. You have complete control over all data generated within the app on your device.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
